The cyp19a1 Gene

Encodes the aromatase enzyme.

The cyp19a1 Gene:

Is involved in gonad differentiation.

The cyp19a1 Gene:

May play a role in sex change.

Using bioinformatics tools...

We will analyze the evolutionary history of this gene and its variants,
comparing the the clownfish to other teleosts.

  • Nucleotide Alignment 1

    We will compare the nucleotide sequences the cyp19a1a gene from a number of teleost species.

  • Nucleotide Alignment 2

    We will compare the nucleotide sequence of the cyp19a1b gene in a number of teleost species.

  • Phylogenetic Tree 1

    We will see how the expression of cyp19a1a, which is expressed in the gonads, has varied throughout time.

  • Phylogenetic Tree 2

    We will see how the expression of cyp19a1b, which is expressed in the gonads, has varied throughout time.

  • Amino Acid Alignment

    We will compare the structure of the aromatase protein throughout teleost species.

  • Phylogenetic Tree

    We will see how this enzyme has been differentially expressed throughout time.
